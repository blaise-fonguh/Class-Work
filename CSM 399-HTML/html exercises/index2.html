Jadon Malik Sancho


Biography

Jadon Malik Sancho is a 22 year old English footballer who plays as winger for English Football Club, Manchester United and the English Football National team.Widely regarded as one of the top wonderkids of his generation, He was born on the 25th of March 2000 and is well-known for his trickery, game intelligence, pace and use of feints in one-on-one situations.Previously a youth player with Watford and Manchester City,Sancho was bought for 21 Million EUR by Dortmund from Manchester City. During his stint at Dortmund,Sancho played exceptionally well and made a world class name for himself earning him an 85 Million EUR move to Manchester United in the summer of 2021 where he plays with the number 25 shirt.
